The only Martin' that could drift em all!

Here is a timeline for The DB5

  • 1963 - went into production
  • 1964 - appeared in the James Bond film Goldfinger
  • 1965 - production stopped with a total of 1,059 units produced
  • 1970 - first reported accident featuring a DB5
  • 1998 - the last functioning DB5 is bought by KHYZYL SALEEM in Germany
  • 2019 - the last time the Aston Martin DB5 was last seen
